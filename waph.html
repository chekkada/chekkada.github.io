Hackathon-1 Overview

The XSS Hackathon, which focuses on Cross-site Scripting, is an educational endeavor aiming at delving into and understanding the various levels of XSS vulnerabilities in online applications. XSS, a common security flaw, allows attackers to inject malicious scripts into web pages that are visible to other users. The hackathon has two main objectives: Task 1 focuses on identifying and exploiting XSS vulnerabilities within a specific online application, whereas Task 2 focuses on implementing preventive measures against XSS assaults to reduce the possibility of exploitation.
